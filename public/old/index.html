Experienced. Professional. Affordable.

Phil Mc Nally is an internationally qualified Interior Designer with over 30 years of experience in her field. She is currently a lecturer in Interior Design and an accreditor of the Griffith College Interior Design Diploma. Phil has worked on hundreds of projects in her time as an interior designer and would love to help you on your next project.

Phil is also a qualified Master of Feng Shui and was trained under the world renowned Denise Linn.

Designs Tailored to Your Needs

The modern world calls for a versatile outlook on interior design and a focus must not only be placed on beautiful and creative designs, but also in comfort and space utilisation.

Cosy Homes

Whether it is a cosy apartment nestled in the City Center, or you are expecting a new arrival, make the most of every inch of your home with stylish and space efficient designs.

Spacious Homes

A home in the Suburbs is like no other. Build your sanctuary and esacpe the hustle and bustle of the City Center. Allow your home to breathe and enjoy the tranquility of a spacious design.

Weddings

Planning your Wedding Day is the most exciting time of your life. Allow yourself to savour the experience with the help of Phil's expertise and enjoy the perfect special day tailored to your specifications, without the stress!

Business

First impressions last and these days the image of your business can win or lose you customers. Ensure professionalism is what comes to mind when a customer visits your place of business.
